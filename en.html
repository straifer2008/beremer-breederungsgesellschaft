SINCE 1970 ON ALL SEAS

BBG - The roots of Bremer Bereederungsgesellschaft date back to 1970 when the company was founded as Frigomaris Shipping. Above all, the company has specialized in the management of container ships since 1987, when it was called Ganymed Shipping.

In 2003, the business activities and the entire team were transferred to Bremer Bereederungsgesellschaft mbH & Co. KG. BBG has extensive experience in technical management, operational management and crew management.

The managed fleet includes modern bulk carriers (Midi, Supramax, Ultramax, Panamax, Post-Panamax and Caper series).

NEWS FROM BBG AND OCEANS

01.09.20 Management without managing director Joachim Zeppenfeld

Joachim Zeppenfeld, as BBG's long-serving managing director and previous minority shareholder, took over all the shares from the previous major shareholder, the Offen Group.

11.12.20 BBG та Harren & Partner join forces

Bremer Bereederungsgesellschaft (BBG) and Harren & Partner (H&P) combine and expand their knowledge, fleets and networks in the bunker segment. The new Harren Bulkers brand provides an umbrella for these joint events.

Bremer Bereederungsgesellschaft and Harren & Partner will operate a fleet of 23 bulk carriers. BBG Managing Director Joachim Zeppenfeld is delighted with the new alliance: “Harren & Partner is a highly diversified family business with a strong presence in the fillers segment, as well as in other markets such as heavy lifts, tankers and container feeders. By joining forces, we can benefit from our shared knowledge while continuing to diversify and strengthen our court management portfolio. BBG will continue to operate as an operating organization, and our customers and business partners will not notice any changes in our day-to-day business. "

The two companies are looking to systematically expand and strengthen the massive segment in the long run: "BBG and H&P see great potential in this area," said Peter Grenvolt, Managing Director of Harren & Partner. “The goal is to continue our strong growth in this sector. The establishment of Harren Bulkers is only the first step - the one that will lay the foundation for the acquisition of new ships and the expansion of our fleet. "

BBG has its roots in 1970, when the company was founded under the name Frigomaris Shipping. The organization has been specializing in container ship management for the first time since Ganymed Shipping since 1987, but BBG has been mainly operating bulk carriers since 2010. The company's fleet includes Midi Supramax, Ultramax, Panamax, Post-Panamax and Caper fillers.

"I am very proud and glad that we managed to create this new union. This is another milestone in the history of Harren & Partner. BBG is a very prestigious company with a long history and experienced employees, as well as a strong international reputation for quality, trust and experience in the bulker segment, ”commented Dr. Martin Harren, Managing Director of Harren & Partner. “Now that Harren & Partner has spent the last few years strengthening and expanding its position in the heavy lift and tanker segments, we want to continue this development with fillers. We are confident that the establishment of Harren Bulkers will put both BBG and Harren & Partner in a very good position for the future. "

Patrick Pucall, Head of Project Finance at H&P and CEO of Harren Bulkers, said the new Brexen Bulkers Alliance is an interesting structure for investors and shipowners: “Our professional ship management operations and efficient financial team make us a particularly attractive partner for investors. We have great group resources for everything we do. We will approach each new project creatively and transparently - and make it successful. "

For more information on Harren Bulkers, visit www.harren-bulkers.de
