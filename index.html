SINCE 1970 AT HOME ON ALL SEAS

BBG - Bremer Bereederungsgesellschaft roots go back to 1970, when the company was founded as „Frigomaris Shipping“. First of all the company has specialised in managing container ships since 1987, when it was called „Ganymed Shipping“.

In 2003 the business activities and the entire team were taken over by Bremer Bereederungsgesellschaft mbH & Co. KG. BBG has a wide range experience in technical management, operational management and also in crew managment.

The managed fleet includes modern bulk carriers (Midi, Supramax, Ultramax, Panamax, Post-Panamax and Caper series).

HIGHEST QUALITY OF SHIP MANAGEMENT

We offer our clients an end-to-end range of services as well as services specially tailored to their requirements. Clients are being kept constantly updated about the financial status of their assets to ensure an utmost transparency of information.

BBG has been a competent partner for newbuilding supervision since 1993. We have successfully taken over the supervision control and monitoring for modern container ships and bulk carriers in several countries, such as Europe, Korea and China.

Thanks to our many years of experience and our local presence with specialists on location we are able to take care of all of our tasks competently from start to finish. The best evidence for this is our growing number of satisfied customers
